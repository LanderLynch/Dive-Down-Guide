Tips & Tricks

Zone Farming

Farm by zone instead of diving randomly. Each biome has its own fish pool, so targeted runs are much more efficient.

Check Rarity Conflicts

When the two sources disagree, treat the fish as a priority to verify in-game before planning a long grind around it.

Late-Game Route

Push your upgrades before farming Deep Dark, Trenches, Atlantis, Shell Reef, and Kraken World to avoid wasted dives.

Permanent progression

Rebirth Guide

Rebirthing in Dive Down gives permanent upgrades without wiping your overall progress. You keep your money, gear, and fish outside of the fish and money consumed by each rebirth requirement.

How Rebirth Works

No Full Reset

Unlike many games, rebirth does not reset your whole account. It only uses the fish and money required for that rebirth tier.

Permanent Perks

Each rebirth increases swim speed, backpack space, and your cash multiplier, making every next farm route faster and richer.

Plan Ahead

Save the required fish before spending or selling them so you can chain rebirths more efficiently when your money is ready.
